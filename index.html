What is Trezor?

Trezor is a hardware wallet that securely stores your cryptocurrency offline, protecting it from hackers and malware. Unlike software wallets, which store private keys on internet-connected devices, Trezor’s offline storage means that your assets remain safe even when your computer is connected to the internet. With Trezor, you can manage multiple cryptocurrencies in one place, enjoying enhanced security and peace of mind.

Getting Started with Trezor at Trézor.io/start

The setup process with Trezor is simple, and it all begins at Trézor.io/start. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting started:

Step 1: Visit Trézor.io/start

Go to Trézor.io/start, the official Trezor setup page. This site provides the latest software and instructions to help you get your Trezor device up and running quickly and securely.

Step 2: Download Trezor Suite

Trezor Suite is the official software for managing your wallet. Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux, this application makes it easy to store, send, and monitor your cryptocurrency. Download and install Trezor Suite from Trézor.io/start to begin.

Step 3: Connect Your Trezor Device

After installing Trezor Suite, connect your Trezor hardware wallet to your computer. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your device, including creating a new wallet or restoring an existing one with your recovery seed.

Step 4: Secure Your Recovery Seed

During the setup, Trezor will generate a recovery seed. Write down this 12- or 24-word phrase and store it in a safe location. Your recovery seed is the only way to restore your wallet if your device is lost or damaged. Never share this information with anyone.
